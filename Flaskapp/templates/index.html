About Dnntal

AI-powered system will read your x-ray image faster with higher accuracy and consistency.

Our computer vision system is intertwined with machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of dental x-ray images.

Features

Pwoerful Features As Always

High Accuracy

We achieved the validation accuracy of 89% in classification and 0.9 in image segmentation.

Consistency

20-40% of misdiagnosis from skilled dental practitioners. We provide consistent results 24/7.

Easy to Use

It's one click away to get a result.

Fully Responsive
